Site Purpose

The Villa Chamber of Commerce serves as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community advocate. We promote businesses through member services, economic and community development, and public policies that balance economic prosperity and promote micro-entrepreneurs."

Scenarios

What’s the best way to network with a chamber of commerce?

The best way to network at a chamber of commerce meeting is by conducting yourself in a professional manner as a representative of your business. It’s recommended that you absorb as much information as possible. Speak to as many people as possible with the goal of making connections and sharing ideas, not just finding your next client. Being actively involved in all chamber activities helps you meet more people, including chamber members and the community.

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

    Food Fair

    The food fair will be a delicious and fun event. We will have a wide variety of food stalls that will offer traditional and exquisite dishes from different cultures. There will also be demonstrations from celebrity chefs, where you can learn cooking techniques and recipe secrets. In addition, there will be interactive activities such as cooking competitions and tastings so you can try different flavors. It will be an unforgettable gastronomic experience for all food lovers and entrepreneurs!

    The Entrepreneurship Conference

    The entrepreneurship conference will be a one-day event full of inspiration and learning. We will have featured speakers sharing their experiences and advice on starting and growing a business. There will also be discussion panels where you can interact with other entrepreneurs and ask questions. In addition, there will be practical workshops where you can acquire specific skills and knowledge. It will be a unique opportunity to connect with the entrepreneurial community and obtain valuable ideas for your own business!

What has been the population growth in the area?

The National Institute of Statistics and Informatics (INEI), on the occasion of the 487th Anniversary of the Foundation of Lima, Ciudad de los Reyes, and according to population estimates and projections as of June 30 of this year, the population of the province of Lima, that is, the 43 districts that make it up, reaches 10 million 4 thousand 141 inhabitants, which represent 29.9% of the projected population of Peru (33 million 396 thousand 698 inhabitants). By sex, in Lima, 52.1% (5 million 220 thousand 755) are women and 47.8% (4 million 783 thousand 386) are men. By large age groups, it can be seen that 64.9% (6 million 493 thousand 809) of the inhabitants of Lima are between 15 and 59 years of age, 18.5% (1 million 850 thousand 730) are 0 to 14 years old and 16.6% (1 million 659 thousand 602) are over 59 years of age.
