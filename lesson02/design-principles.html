Rule of Thirds

Amazon

amazon.com

The Amazon home page uses the rule of thirds to a T. From the top of the home page to the center of the page. Most markets want their consumers to be able to empathize with their brand. By being able to show their consumers all the most popular products they offer in an organized and visually attractive design.

Repetition

Apple

apple.com

Apple home page uses PARC: Repetition to promote the new Apple Watches. In the following example we see the use of the same typography and the repetition of elements, advertising and release date.

Contrast

Maono

maono.com

Maono home page uses PARC: Contrast by using different typography to highlight his product in the middle of the image, he also uses highly contrasting colors such as black, white and yellow, and other details in very bright colors within his product.
